NYC Trees

Modeled a 3D environment of New York City's trees to highlight division within the city and the importance of trees.
Developed a foundational understanding of GIS data, Python in Blender, and working with Princeton's Adroit-Vis cluster.
Documented all work using GitBook and published movie to YouTube.

Softwares: Blender, Python, Premiere Pro, QGIS, R, GitBook
